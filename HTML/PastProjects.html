Fitness Tracking App

In this application, users start on the homepage, where they can answer a few questions to receive recommendations. On the Calories Tracker page, users can input all the food they ate during the day and the corresponding calorie count. At the end of the day, they can hit the "End Day" button, which adds everything from that day to a summary list at the bottom and calculates the total calories. On the Progress page, users can set workout goals and track their current progress. The application will display their progression toward achieving those goals. On the Weight Tracker page, users can update the date and their current weight. These entries are plotted on a graph, which shows a visual summary of their weight changes over time. On the Workout Search page, users can browse a list of workouts, search for a specific workout, or look up exercises based on the muscle group they want to target. Finally, on the Workout Tracker page, users can select a day and log their workouts, including the number of sets and reps performed for each exercise.

Video Rental App

In this app, the user will start off on the home screen and either go to the customer page or the video page. When at the customer page, the user is able to add more customers with all their information. They can also edit any customer from the saved list or delete that customer as well. On the video page, the user can add videos to their list of videos, as well as updating them and deleting them. They can also check out the video of their choosing and attach that checked out video to a certain customer. They can also return the videos with the push of a button, and they can also see who rented the video of their choosing. They also have the ability to search through the movies by searching for any parameter. Throughout both pages with information, the user can save the information to a .txt file and when they reopen the page, the stuff they saved will appear back onto the list.
